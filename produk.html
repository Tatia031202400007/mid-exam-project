Hijab Care Instructions Based on Fabric Type
Fabric Type Washing Instructions Drying Instructions Ironing Instructions
Satin Hand wash with cold water. Dry in a shaded area, avoid direct sunlight. Iron with low heat, avoid direct contact with fabric.
Jersey Machine wash with cold water and mild detergent. Dry in a shaded area, do not wring. Iron with low heat if necessary.
Chiffon Hand wash with gentle liquid soap. Hang dry, avoid wringing. Iron with low heat, avoid excessive heat.
Cotton Machine wash with warm water and mild detergent. Dry in a place with enough sunlight, avoid damp areas. Iron with medium heat, ensure fabric is slightly damp.
